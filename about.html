About Grace Foundation Kenya

Empowering Women, Educating Children, and Providing Access to Clean Water

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable women and children through education, economic support, and access to clean water, creating sustainable and lasting change in rural communities across Kenya.

Our Vision

A world where every woman and child has the opportunity to thrive, free from poverty and with access to the tools they need to succeed.

Our Core Values

  • Empathy: We put ourselves in the shoes of those we serve.
  • Integrity: We are transparent, ethical, and honest in everything we do.
  • Community: We believe in working together to create a better future.
  • Empowerment: We focus on building skills and self-sufficiency for long-term impact.

Our History

Founded in 2015 by Grace Mori, Grace Foundation Kenya began with a mission to help women and children affected by poverty in rural areas. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant community-based organization that supports education, women's economic empowerment, and clean water projects across multiple regions in Kenya.

Our Leadership

Grace Mori

Founder and Director

Grace Mori has dedicated her life to creating opportunities for the most vulnerable. With over 20 years of experience in community work, Grace is committed to empowering women and ensuring that every child has access to education and clean water.
